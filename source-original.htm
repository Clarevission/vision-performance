Our Story

Where Vision Care Meets Workplace Safety

Vision Performance Inc. was founded on a single question that didn't have a clean answer: why are the people whose work depends most on their eyes — pipeliners, machinists, surgeons, drivers, drafters, knowledge workers logging twelve hours a day on a screen — the ones whose eyewear is usually an afterthought? Eye care has historically lived in the optometry clinic. Workplace safety has lived in the HSE office. The eyewear those workers depend on every day is the product of both worlds, yet it's rarely designed with both in mind.

We started this company to close that gap. Today, Vision Performance serves industrial companies, engineering firms, technology organizations and healthcare professionals across Alberta — delivering both performance eyewear and comprehensive mobile optometry services, specified at the intersection of clinical vision care and occupational health and safety.
